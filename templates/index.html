Welcome to

GroundUp

India's tailor-made data collection platform



GroundUp is a digital marketplace and decentralized data collection platform for rural and peri-urban India. We connect data seekers (clients) with trained data collectors (service providers) to facilitate the collection of high quality, local, timely data in large volumes and at low costs.


  • We leverage a hyperlocal fleet of trained data collectors.
  • We use technology to automate aspects of data collection and monitor data quality.
  • We prioritize fair take-home earnings for data collectors.
